How To Play

Setup: The dungeon contains all Clubs/Spades (Monsters), Diamonds 2-10 (Weapons), Hearts 2-10 (Potions). Start at 20 health.

Room: Draw until 4 cards are face up. Each turn, resolve 3 cards and leave 1 for the next room.

Skip: You may skip a room, placing all 4 cards at the bottom, but never twice in a row.

Weapons: Equipping replaces the previous weapon. After a weapon kills a monster, it can only be used on monsters with value ≤ the last monster it killed.

Potions: Use only one potion per turn; extra potions are discarded. Health caps at 20.

Combat: Barehanded: take full monster damage. Weapon: take monster damage minus weapon strength (minimum 0).

End: Win by clearing the dungeon. Lose if health hits 0.
